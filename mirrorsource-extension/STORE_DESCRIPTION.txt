SEE THE WHOLE STORY WITH MIRRORSOURCE

MirrorSource is an AI-powered news intelligence tool that instantly cross-references global news coverage to reveal the consensus facts and key differences behind every headline.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Click & Search: Click the floating "Look for Other Sources" button on any news page, or paste a URL manually.
2. Triangulate: We instantly scan the open web for coverage from major wires (AP, Reuters), public broadcasters (BBC, NPR, CBC), and international outlets (Al Jazeera, DW, France24).
3. Analyze: Our AI generates an Intel Brief showing you the consensus facts and where narratives diverge.
4. Explore: Browse alternative sources with full transparency on ownership and political lean.
